Customer Service

Orders

★ Once I place an order, when will my account be charged?

All orders placed on the Stage Fright Store are immediately charged for the full cost of your order.

★ Can I cancel or make changes to my order?

This depends on where your order is within the shipping progress. If it has not yet been shipped, then it is possible to cancel the order. Please contact us with one of the contacting options listed above to review your request ASAP!

★ What is the sales tax on my order?

The amount taxes depends on the tax laws of the state being ordered from. Sales tax is applied to all orders

★ What do I do if my order arrived damaged, incorrect or incomplete?

Please contact us ASAP to investigate the issue immediately. Additionally, please include pictures of the items you recieved. Upon review of the photos, we’ll issue a return label to you via email.

Returns

★ Will you accept returns of product in any condition?

Returning items must be in their original condition and packaging + tags.

★ Who pays for my return?

For customers ordering from the United States, free return labels will be provided. International customers will need to be responsible for the additional return costs.
